Rules & Regulations :-

1.Roll and soar:- Turn the dice turn your future and embark on a special journey ahead.

2.Turn alternation:- Teams take turn in a clockwise direction to roll the dice and solve mind boggling situations in a limited time.

3.Elementative power:- Each team is granted special elemental powers exclusive only for them. These powers are derived from the elements of Mother Nature that are Fire, Earth, Air and Water.

4.Progressive Moves: As the game unfolds, teams choose their players and execute strategic moves based on the dice roll, advancing their tokens strategically along the path.

5) Special Positions:- There are defined positions on the board reaching upon which each team will get a chance to roll a special dice with infinite powers. The teams will be allowed to use those special powers corresponding to the number on the face of the dice.

6.Brotherhood Move:-Move two players of the rolling team simultaneously.

7.Lovely Push: Move the opponent team 6 blocks back.

8.Blockade:Block the opponent team from using its power on their next special position. .
